The "Hand of God"

Diego Maradona's "Hand of God" moment during the 1986 World Cup remains one of football's most infamous incidents. In a quarter-final match against England, Maradona used his hand to punch the ball into the net, deceiving the referee and scoring a goal. Despite protests from the English players, the goal stood, sparking controversy and debate that continues to this day. Despite the controversy surrounding this incident, Maradona's undeniable talent and impact on the sport remain part of his enduring legacy.
